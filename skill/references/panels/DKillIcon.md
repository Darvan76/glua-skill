# DKillIcon

---

**Hereda de:** `Panel`

---

Uses the killicon to persistently draw a killicon.

---


## Miembros (2)


---

### `DKillIcon:GetName` `[client]`

Gets the killicon being shown.

**Argumentos:**

*(sin argumentos)*

**Retorna:**

- `string` — The name of the killicon currently being displayed.

---

### `DKillIcon:SetName` `[client]`

Sets the killicon to be displayed. You should call Panel:SizeToContents following this.

Killicons can be added with killicon.Add and killicon.AddFont.

**Argumentos:**

- `string iconName` — The name of the killicon to be displayed.

**Retorna:**

*(sin retorno)*
